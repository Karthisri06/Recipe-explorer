Panner Butter Masala

schedule Duration: 30m person Chef: Binaarth restaurantveg star 4.7

Ingredients

  • Vegtable Oil1/2 cup
  • Panner1/2 cup
  • Butter2 tsp
  • Onions2
  • Ginger garlic paste1 tsp
  • Cashews1 tsp
  • Red chiles1 tsp
  • Cumin1/2 tsp
  • Coriander1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala1/2 tsp
  • Tomato sauce2 tsp
  • Milk1/2 cup
  • White sugar1/2 tsp
  • Fresh cream1/2 cup
  • Salt for taste

Directions

Step 1

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat; fry panner in batches until golden, about 5 minutes. Transfer fried panner to a paper towl-lined plate to drain, retaining vegtable oil in skillet.

Step 2

Melt butter in the same skillet over medium heat;

Step 3

Cook and stir onions until golden brown, about 10 minutes. Add ginger garlic paste. Continue to cook until fragrant, about 1 minute more.

Step 4

Stir cashews, red chiles, cumin, coriander and garam masala into the onion mixture. Cook and stir for 10 minutes.

Step 5

Stir tomato sauce, half and cup of milk, sugar, and Salt into spice mixture; simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes.

Step 6

Reduce heat to low. Add fried paneer and simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes more.

Step 7

Serve with fresh cream!.
