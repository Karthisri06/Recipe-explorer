Chicken Biryani

schedule Duration: 1h 12m person Chef: Binaarth restaurantNon-veg star 4.7

Ingredients

  • Chicken1 KG
  • Rice1 KG
  • Large red onions5
  • Peanut oil200 ml
  • Ginger garlic paste2 tsp
  • Ground turmeric1/2 tsp
  • Lemon1
  • Green chillies3
  • Cinnamon sticks2
  • Cumin seeds1 tsp
  • Cloves6
  • Coriander & MintEach 1 bunch
  • Salt for taste

Directions

Step 1

Combine chicken (500g) with yogurt (½ cup), ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt. Marinate for 1-2 hours.

Step 2

Boil water with whole spices. Cook rice until 70% done, then drain.

Step 3

Fry onions until golden brown. Cook marinated chicken for 15 mins.

Step 4

Layer chicken, rice, and fried onions. Add saffron milk and steam (dum) for 20-25 minutes.

Step 5

Let rest, mix gently, and serve hot with raita or salad.
